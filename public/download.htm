Prime Video


Prime Video is a streaming TV and film service from Amazon.

Launched in 2014, it allows Prime members to watch TV via an internet connection, without having to download. If you’re using a tablet or mobile device, you can also download episodes to watch later.

You don’t buy individual series or films on Amazon Prime Video. Instead, you pay a monthly subscription which gives you access to a whole library of programmes to watch. Any programme that is included in the membership will have a Prime symbol over the image in search results.

With an eligible Amazon Prime Video membership, you will have access to thousands of Prime Video titles at no additional cost.

You will also have the option to rent or buy movies and TV episodes.

The membership includes a huge library of movies and TV shows across every category, from romantic comedies to timeless classics.

Amazon also shares a handful of popular and award-winning Amazon Original Movies and Prime Original series. Prime Video offers digital movies and TV shows you can watch instantly on compatible smart Mitchell & Brown TVs.

The service is free for Amazon Prime members (if you have free delivery, you probably have Prime), and all the shows are available through the Amazon website, so if you’ve used it for shopping then the service will be familiar.

*Subscription required.
